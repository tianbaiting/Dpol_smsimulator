\section*{Scope}
The y-pol sample is \texttt{data/qmdrawdata/qmdrawdata/20260413ypol/}: $^{112}$Sn and $^{124}$Sn at 190 MeV/u, $\gamma=0.50\ldots0.85$, ynp/ypn, RP360. Each \texttt{reactiontype.dat} row contributes one ImQMD event. The denominator is the total number of rows in that file.
The z-pol Sn112/Sn124 b-discrete files do not carry \texttt{reactiontype.dat} labels.  For z-pol, this note reports $P_{\mathrm{breakup}}(b)=N_{\mathrm{dbreak}}(b)/N_{\mathrm{header}}(b)$ from \texttt{dbreakbXX.dat}; it is not the same observable as the y-pol reaction-class fraction.
\section*{y-pol reaction categories}
The four labels present in the files are \texttt{dkeep}, \texttt{break}, \texttt{spall}, and \texttt{other}. For Sn112 the largest break fraction is 45.1\% at g080 ynp; for Sn124 it is 50.5\% at g085 ypn.

\section*{z-pol breakup probability}
The largest z-pol b-discrete probability in the scanned Sn112 files is 100.0\% at g100 zpn, b=10 fm. For Sn124 the largest value is 99.9\% at g100 zpn, b=10 fm.
